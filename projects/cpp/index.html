Sierpinski Triangle
The Sierpinski Triangle is a fractal in the shape of an triangle, further divided into smaller triangles. Here's how my version works:

  1. Input the desired number of iterations n.
  2. Creates a triangle of height 2n
  3. The central triangle is removed after dividing the triangle into 4.
  4. Step 3 is now carried out on the other congruent triangles, resulting in more triangles with half the height.
  5. Step 4 is repeated recursively until further iterations would create triangles of a height lower than 2.
